About us

Building the future of Australian business software

NewEraTechServices is an Australian technology company on a mission to give every industry purpose-built software — not generic tools that don't fit.

Our mission

Software that works the way your industry actually works

Most business software forces you to adapt your operations to fit the tool. We believe it should be the other way around. Every RaddeCore product is built ground-up for its industry — with the terminology, compliance rules and workflows your team already knows.


We started with NDIS — because we saw firsthand how SCHADS Award complexity, NDIS billing rules and compliance requirements were breaking providers who deserved better tools. Then restaurants. Then retail. Each vertical gets the same treatment: deep domain knowledge translated into software that just works.

Our story

How we got here

From a single-file HTML app to a multi-vertical enterprise platform.

2022
2022
The beginning
NewEraTechServices was founded in Australia with a simple idea: build business software that actually fits the industry it serves. First project — a retail ERP for a small cosmetics business.
2023
2023
RaddeCore platform born
Rebuilt from scratch as a multi-tenant SaaS platform with Fastify, PostgreSQL and React. Launched the retail vertical with hospitality features including restaurant POS, table management and kitchen display.
2024
2024
RaddeCare+ launches
Identified the massive gap in NDIS practice management software. Built the complete SCHADS Award engine (152+ rate entries, all 4 streams), NDIS billing, compliance management and Xero integration from the ground up.
2025
2025
Three verticals. One platform.
RaddeCare+ (NDIS), RaddeServe (Restaurant) and RaddeOne (Retail) are live and serving customers across Australia. The platform now handles SCHADS payroll, NDIS billing, KDS, floor plans, double-entry accounting and more.
