FAQ

Common questions

How long does it take to get started?
Most customers are up and running within a few hours of signing up. Our onboarding wizard walks you through setup step by step. No implementation consultants needed.
Can I migrate data from my existing system?
Yes. RaddeCore has a built-in data migration engine that supports CSV, Excel and JSON imports for participants, workers, products, customers and more.
Do you offer custom pricing for large organisations?
Yes. Contact our sales team for enterprise pricing, volume discounts, custom SLAs and dedicated onboarding support.
Is RaddeCare+ compliant with the NDIS Price Guide?
Yes. RaddeCare+ is designed specifically for registered NDIS providers and is updated with each NDIS Price Arrangement and Price Limits release.
Where is my data stored?
All data is stored in Australia on enterprise-grade infrastructure. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Multi-tenant isolation ensures your data is never accessible to other organisations.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes — every plan includes a 7-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You can also explore the platform on our fully interactive live demo at demo.raddecore.com.
